Cornell Extracurriculars

Discrete Structures (CS2800) Course Consultant
January 2021 - Present

  • Worked under Professor Anke van Zuylen for the Spring 2021 semester; Professor Frans Schalekamp and Alexandra Silva for the Fall 2021 semester
  • Lead 2 virtual discussion sections for more than 40 students by preparing slide decks to review lecture material and facilitating breakout rooms for students to solve problems together
  • Grade weekly homework assignments and exams on mathematical induction; logical proof; propositional and predicate calculus; combinatorics and discrete mathematics; some basic elements of basic probability theory; basic number theory; sets, functions, and relations; graphs; and finite-state machines
