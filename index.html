Hi, I'm Winston!

Thanks for stopping by and learning more about me!

I am originally from Long Island though I have begun to call Ithaca, NY home as well. Right now, I'm a junior in the College of Engineering at Cornell University studying Computer Science and Operations Research. These two fields interested me because they have the potential to help innovate technology faster in order to make the world into a better place.

Although I don't know what I want to do for the future, I am interested in learning more about software engineering, product management, and quantitative research (particularly in the financial and healthcare industry). I hope to do so by interning during Summer 2022, so I can get a better understanding of the challenges and opportunities that exist in these industry.

Before you click around on my website, I would love to share some facts about myself so we can know each other better!

1. Last summer (2021), I interned at a MedTech startup as a PM. It was a great experience as it gave me exposure to Agile and the healthcare industry. My internship here has made me more curious about the potential technology has in improving patient outcomes as it seems as if this industry is technologically lagging compared to others. For example, in the FinTech world, there have been leaders that have emerged as the de facto platform. I think it will be interesting to see who in healthcare can innovate the fastest to provide patients and providers with a tool that is secure and easy to access/manage.

2. I enjoy living an active lifestyle and enjoy playing various types of sports. When the weather is nice, tennis and basketball are the ones I typically play the most, though I do play golf occasionally. During the winter, I love going snowboarding as the crisp mountain air really energizes me. And at Cornell, I learned how to rock climb and am belay certified - rock climbing is the one sport where I'll say it is harder than it looks!

3. My parents moved to America from Taiwan and I still have family there. The background photo on my website is actually from my grandparents' home in the New Taipei district. Aside from spending time with them (particularly playing Mahjong), I also love Taiwanese cuisine. There are so many interesting foods at the night market and I probably spend an unhealthy amount of time there when I visit. Xiaolongbao or soup dumplings are my favorite and Din Tai Fung does a great job of making them, so I'm super jealous in the US, they are only available on the West Coast.
