Hi, thanks for stopping by!

My name is Winston and I am currently a Site Reliability Engineer for Salesforce's Government Cloud. I am also a Master's Student at Georgia Institute of Technology where I study Analytics. Prior to this, I did my undergraduate at Cornell University in Ithaca, New York and received my degree in Computer Science with a minor in Operations Research from the College of Engineering.

Here are some of my hobbies when I am not working or studying:

1. Growing up, I was a big tennis player. Though I still play for fun, I have added pickleball to the mix. You can also catch me playing pickup basketball and some two hand touch football. Aside from sports, I do enjoy spending time outdoors. Ever since my trip to Norway and Sweden, I have started to go on more hikes and kayaking. Staying active and reconnecting with nature is one of my ways to wind down!

2. I'm not quite a big foodie, but I can always appreciate trying out new cuisines and restaurants. I love Taiwanese food, especially soup dumplings and beef wrapped in a scallion pancake. While at Cornell, I took HADM 4300 (Introduction to Wines) and nowadays, I try to apply my classroom knowledge at the wine store. Yes, we do more than just drink in that class! My favorite wines include Riesling from the Finger Lakes and Cabernet Sauvignon from Napa Valley. I have really enjoyed trying to find wines to pair with my meals.

3. My parents immigrated from Taiwan and I still have family who lives there. So, Taiwan especially New Taipei is somewhere that I frequently travel to. Some of my favorite memories there include exploring food stalls and playing arcade games at the night markets. Additionally, there is a lot of beauty in Southern Taiwan and plenty of opportunities to walk around national parks and drink local tea. It is truly an underrated country and I highly recommend adding it to your travel list - there is something for everyone!

4. When I am feeling like a couch potato, you can catch me watching sports. Being raised in NY, I am quite passionate about the local teams even though they have resulted in a lot of heartbreaks. My favorite teams are the New York Knicks and New York Mets - I hope for better days for them. Prior to his retirement, Roger Federer was my favorite player, but I still actively follow the tennis grand slams.
