Winston Liu

Computer Science @ Cornell Engineering

Hi, thanks for stopping by!

My name is Winston and I am a senior at Cornell's College of Engineering where I major in Computer Science and minor in Operations Research/Management Science. By studying these two areas, I have become interested in designing and applying algorithms to solve technical challenges as well as using data (whether it is producing visualizations or creating predictive models) to analyze problems faced by businesses. Aside from working on a variety of projects at Cornell, my professional experiences include interning at Salesforce as a Site Reliability Engineer and BrightInsight, a digital health startup, as a Program Manager.

But, here are some fun facts about myself that is not on my resume :)

1. I love to move around and be active! This includes playing tennis, basketball, or football (non-tackle of course) with my friends. At Cornell, I have also picked up rock climbing so you might catch me bouldering at Lindseth Climbing Center sometimes. In the winter time, I enjoy spending time on the mountains where I go snowboarding and indulging sweets from the Waffle Cabin. Catch me riding a balance board in the office if there's one available!

2. I don't quite consider myself to be a foodie since I don't believe in the "camera eats first," however a lot of my friends do ask me for recommendations. Nonetheless, I do enjoy trying out new cuisines and am pretty lucky that The Establishment restaurant exists on our campus because some of my fellow Cornellians know how to cook and make their own menu. My go to drink is passion fruit green tea with boba and I am a huge fan of scallion pancakes, soup dumplings, and sushi!

3. My parents are originally from Taiwan and it has been one of my favorite places to visit. Obviously having family there makes it great, but there is so much to do from exploring the modern city of Taipei to the natural scenery in the South. I enjoy going to Tamsui the most because of the beautiful river and the plethora of food options from the night market (ok maybe I am a foodie). If you want any recommendations on things to do in Taiwan, feel free to reach out as I am more than happy to share must visit places to get the best food.

4. Aside from playing sports, I do enjoy watching basketball, hockey, and tennis. I am a huge supporter of the New York Knicks and Mets where I hope each season for a championship. My favorite tennis player is Roger Federer and I was able to see him play live in Arthur Ashe a few years ago. But, the team you can constantly see me cheer in person for is the Cornell Men's Hockey Team - go Big Red! I always love the energy in Lynah Rink and the traditions behind the student section.
