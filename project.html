Cornell Hyperloop

Cornell Hyperloop is a Cornell University Project Team, and our mission is to contribute to the development of Hyperloop. Hyperloop is an Elon Musk proposed mode of passenger and freight transportation, a frictionless, vacuum-sealed tube system through which a pod travels at speeds exceeding 600 miles per hour.

Academic Year 2020-2021
In my first year working as a Hardware Programmer, I was assigned to the Sensors Subteam. I collaborated with two other members where we utilized an Agile work environment to organize our sprints objectives. These included researching sensors such as pressure and ultra-range finders which would collect data and help our entire team to determine the health of our pod. At the end of the year, I was able to produce many individual sensor research document as well as a master code. My code utilized all the sensors and read in measurements which was sent to a Master Arduino via a Transmitter Receiver module and then acted appropriately according to our State Diagram.
